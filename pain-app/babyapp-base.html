WebMD Baby Features

Growth Tracker

At each "well visit," take a photo, enter and track your baby's height, weight, and head circumference, and use the child growth calculator to determine growth percentile of children the same age.

Diaper Tracker

Record the date and time of every diaper change, and note whether the diaper was wet, dirty, or both. You can email each diaper entry, generate a daily diaper change report, or see all changes in a calendar view.

Sleep Tracker

Record the date, time, and length of your baby's sleep sessions, tracking details like whether the baby slept in a crib or a car seat. You can email each sleep entry, generate a daily sleep report, or see all sleep sessions in a calendar view.

Feeding Tracker

Record the date, time, and source for every feeding, and set up a reminder for the next feeding. You can email each feeding entry, generate a daily report of all feedings, or view all feedings on a calendar.

Baby Book

Capture and record a baby's key memories and milestones in the Baby Book. Add photos or videos with custom captions and assign them to the Baby Book by milestone, then share each memory via email and Facebook.
